About

I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), studying Computer Science and Linguistics. My major allows me to explore the structure, processing, and theory of language while integrating computer science techniques. So far, I have completed courses in algorithms, data structures, and artificial intelligence on the computer science side, while also studying syntax, phonetics, semantics, and language structure in linguistics.

Born in San Francisco, CA, and raised in the Mission District, I take pride in being a Bay Area native. As a Latina and Pacific Islander, growing up in a diverse cultural background has been both enriching and eye-opening, shaping my perspective on the world and the way I approach problem-solving.

My journey into computer science began in middle school when I was first introduced to programming through simple Scratch blocks and an Arduino car. What started as an after-school activity quickly became a passion, evolving into the career path I am now pursuing.
